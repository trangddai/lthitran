Welcome to my growth!

I'm excited to take you on a journey through my educational and professional milestones. This timeline page serves as a testament to my dedication, growth, and continuous learning. So, join me as we navigate through the significant moments that have shaped my knowledge and expertise.

2016

High School Graduation and Asia Travel Adventure

High school graduation marked a significant milestone in my life, and I decided to embark on an exciting journey of exploration and cultural immersion. After completing my studies, I set off on a year-long adventure across various Asian countries. I had the wonderful opportunity to visit Korea, Taiwan, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, China, and Hong Kong. It was an incredible chapter in my life that allowed me to grow as an individual and appreciate the beauty of our diverse world.

2017

My first job at a boba tea shop

After returning from my amazing journey through Asia, I was ready to kickstart my professional career. I landed my first job at a vibrant boba tea shop. This journey from being an employee to a store manager was both challenging and rewarding. It allowed me to develop valuable skills in leadership, team management, and customer relations. The experience taught me the importance of effective communication, problem-solving, and adaptability in a fast-paced environment.

2018

Moving to the U.S. with my Family: A New Chapter Begins

Moving to a new country is an significant milestone in life. The transition, while challenging at times, also contains countless moments of growth, resilience, and adaptation. We navigate the process of integrating into a new community, finding a place to call home, and settling in a foreign land. I am grateful for the opportunities that I have been given at both personal and professional growth.

2019 - Present

Embarking on ESL and A.A.S Degree at Northern Virginia Community College

By pursuing my studies in ESL and an A.A.S degree at NOVA, I had the opportunity to lay a solid foundation for my future academic goals and career opportunities. The knowledge, skills and experience gained during my time at the school have equipped me with the necessary tools to excel in my chosen field and make a meaningful contribution to society.

2020

Started my job at warehouse in Springfield

The fast-paced nature of the warehouse environment taught me the importance of attention to detail, organization, and the ability to work effectively under pressure. I embraced the opportunity to develop my problem-solving skills, adaptability, and time management capabilities. The challenges presented in this role allowed me to grow both personally and professionally.
